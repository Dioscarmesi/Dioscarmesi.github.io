---------------------------------------English---------------------------------------
Thank you for your feedback. We have made several changes to the project:

- The range of Pickups has been adjusted.
- The trigger area of the chairs has been modified.
- The Raygun Shrink has been added (to grow or shrink in size).
- Two functional buttons have been added (the first column from the left) that correctly turn off the functions of the Grab system. There is a third button, but it is still being tested.
- Collisions of the options menu have been modified.

We are continuing to work on a Climbing system that is fully functional for both PC and Quest.
