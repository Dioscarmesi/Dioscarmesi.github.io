- Toggles were added to activate or deactivate the Scaling system.
- A "Grab System" toggle was added (enabled by default, allowing other users to grab you).
- A "Pick Escape" toggle was added (enabled by default, allowing you to escape if another player has grabbed you).
- A "TP Elevators" toggle was added (enabled by default, providing access to teleporters).
- A "Collision" toggle was added (enabled by default, activating collisions for certain objects).
- A "Chair" toggle was added (enabled by default, granting access to all chairs from pickups).
- Some lightgroups were adjusted (some users appeared too dark).
- Some objects were removed and replaced.
- More mirrors were added to the world.
Other updates:
- Work is ongoing to implement the climbing system.
- Some toggles are being analyzed for convenience (some toggles may change to disabled by default in the future).
- Chairs are still being tested.
